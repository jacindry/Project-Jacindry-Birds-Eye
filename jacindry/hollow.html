Hollow Prosthesis

Hollow Prosthesis is an ocular prosthesis that is used to restore the appearance of the eye after the removal of the natural eye. It is a shell-like prosthesis that is custom-made to fit the shape of the patient’s socket. Hollow Prosthesis is recommended for cases where the eye has been removed due to trauma, infection, or cancer.

The Hollow Prosthesis is made of medical-grade acrylic material and is designed to be lightweight, durable, and comfortable to wear. The prosthesis is carefully crafted to match the remaining natural eye in terms of size, shape, and colour. It is then placed into the patient’s socket and secured in place with a special adhesive.

At the Centre for Artificial Eye, we specialize in creating custom Hollow Prostheses that are tailored to each patient’s unique needs. We will work closely with each patient to ensure that their prosthesis fits comfortably and looks natural. We use the latest techniques and materials to create prostheses that are not only functional but also aesthetically pleasing.
