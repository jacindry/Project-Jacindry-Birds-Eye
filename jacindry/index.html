ABOUT US

Centre for Artificial Eye, India’s leading centre for custom-made ocular prostheses. With over 15 years of experience, we have been committed to providing our patients with optimal cosmetic prosthetic rehabilitation following enucleation or evisceration, and orbit following exenteration.

We specialize in a variety of services, including Ocular Prosthetics, Scleral Shell, Orbital Prosthesis (Silicone),Spectacle Mounted Ocular, Hollow Prosthesis, Microphthalmies Treatment, Management of Contracted Socket, Pressure Conformer, Innovative Conformers, Crutch Glasses.

KNOW YOUR OCULARIST

Raju Kumar is an experienced Ocularist and Anaplastologist with over 15 years of experience in the field. He has completed his long-term fellowship He has done Observership in NHS Glasgow and Vice President of the Ocularist Society of India.

Kumar began his career as a consultant ocularist at LVPEI Hyderabad and later founded the ocularistry services at the Centre for Sight Hyderabad. He has served more than 5000 patients with various ocular prosthetic needs. Dr. Kumar is highly regarded in his field and is a visiting faculty member at several other oculoplastic centers in India and abroad.

With his expertise in ocular prosthetics, Dr. Kumar has helped many patients achieve a natural and comfortable look, restoring their confidence and quality of life. His dedication to his profession, commitment to excellence, and compassionate care make him a trusted and respected Ocularist in the industry.
